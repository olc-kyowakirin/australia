XLH Australia and Kyowa Kirin Australia collaborate in first-ever,
Australia-based patient impact survey.

28 February 2022


New research revealing the extent of the physical, emotional, and social burden of X-linked hypophosphataemia (XLH) on Australian patients1 been released to mark Rare Disease Day (Monday, 28 February).

The first-ever, Australia-based research from XLH Australia Incorporated (in collaboration with Kyowa Kirin Australia) aims to increase understanding of what it is like to live with the rare genetic disease.1
